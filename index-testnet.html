🎡 What is LUKSO Fair?

LUKSO Fair is a blockchain carnival built on LUKSO. Play games to win unique NFTs, tokens and LYX!

🎮 How to play

  • Connect your Universal Profile
  • Each spin costs 0.1 LYX
  • Win NFTs, sLYX, PHLAME, AGENTPO or free spins
  • Use free spin tokens (🎟️) for free spins
  • Set a session limit for responsible play

🏆 Prize tiers

  • 🏆 Mythic NFT — 3× LUKSO logo
  • 💜 Rare NFT — 3× UP logo
  • 🌟 Uncommon NFT — 3× Emmet
  • 🖼️ Common NFT — 2× rare symbol
  • 💰 LYX tokens — 3× Bills or Coins
  • 🎟️ Free spins — 3× Free Spin symbol
  • 🎟️ Consolation spin — every 5 spins

🔒 Fairness

  • All spins use a commit-reveal system on LUKSO blockchain
  • Results cannot be influenced by owner or player
  • Prize pool is locked in the smart contract
