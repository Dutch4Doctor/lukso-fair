What is LUKSO Fair?

LUKSO Fair is a community-driven blockchain carnival built on LUKSO. Play games, win unique NFTs, tokens and LYX!

How to play

  • Connect your Universal Profile
  • Each spin costs 0.1 LYX
  • Win NFTs, sLYX, PHLAME, AGENTPO or free spins
  • Use free spin tokens (🎟️) for free spins
  • Set a session limit for responsible gaming

Prizes

  • 🏆 Mythic NFT — 3× LUKSO logo
  • 💜 Rare NFT — 3× UP logo
  • 🌟 Uncommon NFT — 3× Emmet
  • 🖼️ Common NFT — 2× rare symbol
  • 💰 LYX — 3× Bills or Coins
  • 🎟️ Free spins — every 5 spins guaranteed

Fairness
