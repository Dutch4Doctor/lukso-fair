ℹ️ Game Rules

How to Play

  • Connect your Universal Profile to start playing.
  • Each spin costs 0.1 LYX.
  • Spin the reels and match symbols to win prizes.
  • Collect your winnings at any time using the Collect button.

Prizes — 3× Same Symbol

  • 🏆 3× LUKSO → Mythic NFT (1 of 1)
  • ⭐ 3× UP → Rare NFT
  • 🎴 3× Emmet → Uncommon NFT
  • 🎴 3× Elyx → Common NFT
  • 🔥 3× Phlamey → PHLAME tokens
  • 🤖 3× Agent → AGENTPO tokens
  • 💎 3× sLYX → sLYX tokens
  • 💰 3× Bills → 0.20 LYX
  • 💰 3× Coins → 0.05 LYX
  • 🎟️ 3× Free Spins → 5 free spins

Free Spins

  • 2× Free Spins symbol → 3 free spins
  • 2× Any other matching symbol → 1 free spin
  • Free spins do not cost LYX.

NFT Prizes

  • NFTs are awarded from the LUKSO Fair Prizes Season 1 collection.
  • Each tier has a limited supply. When a tier is empty, that prize is temporarily unavailable.
  • NFTs are sent directly to your Universal Profile.

Responsible Gaming

  • You can set a session spending limit before each session.
  • When your limit is reached, the spin button is blocked.
  • Only play with amounts you can afford to lose.
  • LUKSO Fair is intended for entertainment purposes only.

Fairness

  • All spins use a commit-reveal scheme on the LUKSO blockchain.
  • Results cannot be manipulated by the house or the player.
  • Prize pool distribution: 1% platform fee, 49.5% token prizes, 49.5% NFT reserve.
  • The prize pool is locked on-chain and cannot be withdrawn by the owner.
